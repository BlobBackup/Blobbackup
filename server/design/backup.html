Backup New Computer

Step 1: Download Blobbackup onto your computer.
Step 2: Run the .dmg file and drag the Blobbackup app to your applications folder.
Step 3: Open the Blobbackup app from your applications folder.
Step 4: Sign in to your Blobbackup account.
Step 5: Follow the instruction on your screen until you reach this window.
Step 6: You're done! Your computer is now being backed up. You can change the settings anytime by opening the Blobbackup control panel from the menu bar icon.
